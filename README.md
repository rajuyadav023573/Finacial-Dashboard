# Financial Dashboard – Sales & Profit Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes financial performance data using Power BI to track sales, profit,
costs, and product performance across different years, countries, and business segments.
The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor business performance and make data-driven
financial decisions.

## Project Objectives
- Analyze total sales, profit, and cost of goods sold (COGS)
- Track financial performance by product and segment
- Compare sales and profit across countries
- Analyze monthly and yearly sales trends
- Support financial and business decision-making

## Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Measures and KPIs)
- Data Cleaning and Preparation
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
- Financial Analytics

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Units Sold
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Total Products

## Dashboard Insights
- Paseo and VTT products generate the highest sales and profit
- Government and Small Business segments contribute the most revenue
- United States and Canada show strong sales performance
- Monthly analysis highlights peak sales and profit periods
- Year-wise comparison shows overall business growth

## Project Files
- FINANCIAL DASHBOARD.pbix – Power BI dashboard file
- Finacial Dashboard.pdf – Dashboard export

## Conclusion
This Power BI financial dashboard provides a clear view of business performance by
analyzing sales, profit, and costs, enabling better financial planning and strategy.
